Our Story

Orasimos Beekeeping is a family-run apiary established in 2024 on the beautiful island of Crete. Our journey into the world of beekeeping began with a profound passion for nature and a deep love for bees. We are dedicated to producing the finest organic honey and a wide range of bee-related products. Our commitment to sustainability and environmentally-friendly practices ensures that our bees thrive in their natural habitat, allowing us to deliver products that are both high in quality and beneficial to the ecosystem. Join us in celebrating the rich tradition of beekeeping and the wonders of nature.

From the moment we started, our focus has been on maintaining the purity and natural essence of our honey. We believe in the importance of preserving biodiversity and supporting local flora and fauna. By situating our apiaries in remote, pristine locations, we minimize exposure to pollutants and maximize the health benefits of our honey. Our Mission

Our mission at Orasimos Beekeeping is to provide high-quality, natural honey and bee products that promote health and well-being. We strive to preserve the natural habitat of our bees, ensuring they thrive in a healthy and balanced environment. By using sustainable and environmentally-friendly practices, we guarantee that our products are pure and free from pollutants. Our apiaries are located in remote, pristine areas of Crete, allowing our bees to flourish in their natural surroundings. We offer a range of products, including honey, propolis, royal jelly, and beeswax, all crafted with care and dedication. Our commitment to quality and sustainability reflects our deep respect for nature and our desire to share its benefits with our customers. Join us in our mission to support both your health and the environment.

Meet the Family

At Orasimos Beekeeping, our family is at the heart of everything we do. We work together to manage our hives, harvest honey, and produce a variety of bee products. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every jar of honey and every product we create.

We believe that the care and passion we put into our work directly translates into the quality of our products. Each family member plays a vital role, from tending to the bees to ensuring that our production processes are sustainable and environmentally friendly. This collaborative effort ensures that we deliver the finest honey and bee products to our customers, promoting health and well-being while also respecting and preserving nature. Our family values are embedded in our business, driving us to continuously improve and innovate in our beekeeping practices.

In addition to our dedication to quality, we are also deeply committed to educating our customers about the benefits of our products and the importance of bees in our ecosystem. Through workshops, tours, and community outreach, we aim to raise awareness about sustainable beekeeping and the critical role bees play in maintaining biodiversity. By choosing Orasimos Beekeeping, you are not only enjoying premium bee products but also supporting a family business that prioritizes environmental stewardship and community engagement.

Our Products

From aromatic thyme honey to rich carob honey, our range of products showcases the diverse flavors of Crete. We also offer propolis, royal jelly, beeswax candles, and more. Each product is carefully crafted to ensure the highest quality and taste.

Our thyme honey, harvested in summer, boasts a robust, aromatic flavor and golden hue, considered a luxury in Greece. Carob honey, with its unique earthy taste, transforms into a mellow, rich "brown gold" as it matures. Explore our full product line to discover the best of what Crete's flora has to offer.

Whether you're looking for nutritious bee pollen, soothing sage honey, or eco-friendly beeswax wraps, our products are designed to meet a variety of needs while maintaining the highest standards of purity and sustainability. Experience the excellence of Orasimos Beekeeping, where tradition meets innovation.
